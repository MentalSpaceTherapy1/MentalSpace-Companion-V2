Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 22, 2025

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

Summary: MentalSpace Companion is a mental health support app. We collect only the data necessary to provide personalized mental wellness guidance. We never sell your data. You can delete your data at any time.

1. Introduction

MentalSpace Companion ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mental health companion application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Check-in Data: Daily mental health metrics you submit (mood, stress, sleep, energy, focus, anxiety levels)
  • Journal Entries: Optional written reflections you choose to save
  • Care Preferences: Your preferred support style and mental health goals
  • Weekly Focus: Focus areas and intentions you set
  • Action Completions: Records of wellness activities you complete

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features accessed, check-in frequency, and app engagement patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Timestamps: When you use various features of the app

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Full chat transcripts or conversation history
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or social connections
  • Financial or payment information
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide Personalized Support: Generate tailored action plans and recommendations based on your check-ins
  • Track Progress: Show you trends, streaks, and insights about your mental wellness journey
  • Improve the Service: Understand usage patterns to enhance features (using aggregated, anonymized data only)
  • Communicate with You: Send important service updates or respond to support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure
  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to essential service operations
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted providers who help operate our service (e.g., Firebase for hosting) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., sharing a weekly summary card)

6. Third-Party Integrations

When you use MentalSpace Companion through ChatGPT or other AI platforms:

  • We receive only your user identifier and tool requests
  • We do not receive or store your full conversation history
  • Data shared with the AI platform is governed by their respective privacy policies

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional features or notifications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Data Retention

  • We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Check-in history is retained to provide trend analysis and insights
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for service improvement

9. Children's Privacy

MentalSpace Companion is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Crisis and Safety Information

Important: MentalSpace Companion is a wellness support tool, not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you are in crisis:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: 911

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy with an updated effective date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. International Users

Our services are hosted in the United States. If you access the service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US where data protection laws may differ from your jurisdiction.
